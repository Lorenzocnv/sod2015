LOD events eXplorer

A simple explorer of Trentino's events. The project started during the 2015 edition of Spaghetti Open Data meeting in the context of the Fusepool P3 European project. To retrieve information related to a specific event, you can click on the calendar and/or on the timeline below. This visualization is powered by Uduvudu, an extensible and adaptive RDF User Interface engine.
